FAQ

When does LOGR launch?
We're targeting a public launch window in 2026. Join the waitlist on the homepage to get launch timing and early access details as we open spots.
What can I track in LOGR?
Routines and sessions, sets and reps, volume, PRs, and weekly trends shown next to your feed so progress isn't buried in menus. Share session recaps and clips with your crew when you want the room to see it.
How does the crew feed work?
Your feed is built for training: who showed up, what they logged, PRs, and short clips so accountability lives where you already scroll. Comment and hype your people without splitting across another group chat.
Who is LOGR for gyms, teams, or solo lifters?
Anyone who trains can log solo, but LOGR shines when you train with people: lifting partners, gym crews, student athlete rooms, or coaches who want visibility without juggling five apps.
What devices does LOGR support?
LOGR is designed mobile first, the experience you saw in the demo is meant for your phone on the gym floor. Web support lets you browse on desktop when you're planning or reviewing the week.
How do I get access?
Add your email on the homepage waitlist. We'll share launch updates and how to claim your spot as we roll out founding access.
